#Relationship Manager 
**WHO**
*I perform sales of holiday packages
*I would like to understand the customer and their needs
*Employee involved in receiving calls to speak and sell travel packages to customers
*I hold product knowledge
*My personal profile is matched to specific customer needs to sell products more effectively
*I take a 10 minute questionnaire when getting hired which gets used to build my profile and skill matrix.

**DO**
*I need to adjust to each customer’s needs based on their scenario
*Success criteria = I will be successfully rerouted to a customer who matches my skills and knowledge.
*I want to fulfil my customers’ needs and wants
*Success criteria = happy customers, positive feedback
*I want to sell our products efficiently
*Success criteria = I sell a product, increased sale

**THINK/FEEL**
*Pains:*
*The system might fail if not correctly implemented
*I might get incorrectly matched with a customer
*I won’t be able to effectively sell the product through this system
*I have privacy concerns regarding sharing so many details about myself to create my profile (e.g. sex, culture, etc.)
*I will need to learn a complete new system to try and help customers that may end up failing
*There could be misunderstandings with the customer over the phone

*Gains:*
*I want to be able to sell products to the customer to make them happy
*I want communication between customers and other RMs to be easy and efficient
*I want to be matched properly with the right customers based on my expertise and skills

**SEE**
*I see opportunity for career growth, skills development, experience
*I am seeing colleagues speaking with their clients
*I see the equipment that we use for our job and the more advanced equipment that we will be using
*I see others agreeing that the system will ease our work and can increase sales
*I am reading product information to increase my product knowledge and gain more customers
*I am watching the customer base extend as the system is allowing for more callers efficiently

**HEAR** 
*I am hearing others say there will be difficulties in adjusting to the new system
*I am hearing people complaining about injustices in measuring their skills and performance levels
*I am hearing that the system is being developed
*I am hearing the boss mention that sales will increase
*My colleagues are saying that this system is advanced and that it is nice to see the company implementing this technology
*My colleagues are saying that this system will change the work dynamic we currently have

**SAY**
*Customers can be hard to deal with in the current system
*The previous knowledge I had regarding the flow of the system will not help me
*Their previous experiences or ethics can motivate their behaviour, as well as, the possibility for new opportunities

#End-customers
**WHO**
*I want to understand how to communicate with the relationship manager
*I will hang up when I have to wait for too long
*If I have a high score, I will be served first
*I am trying to purchase the travel packages

**DO**
*I need to be able to speak to the RM to enquire about my purchase
*The system will be successful for me if I manage to easily purchase the product
*I would prefer individual support for my needs and requests
*I need to decide if the RM is suitable for my needs
*I will need to dial a number to reach the CMC

**THINK/FEEL**
*Pains:*
*I am worried that I will not get the product I enquired for
*The RM won't understand my needs
*There could be misunderstandings with the RM over the phone

*Gains:* 
*I want helpful and quick service
*I want to be able to be connected with a highly skilled and knowledgeable RM
*I want to speak with an RM that understands my specific needs
*I want a fast response time

**SEE**
*I see many products in the catalogue/advertising offered by the company
*I see many other customers going through this travel company with positive reviews
*I see mainly positive reviews from the company
*I see a range of products being offered

**HEAR**
*My friends have told me that the CMC is excellent 
*I am hearing others say they have good reputation
*My family really liked their friendly service
*I hear that the system will greatly improve the efficiency  that the RM helps me through my request

**SAY**
